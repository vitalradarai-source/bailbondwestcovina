Where West Covina Arrests Are Processed

Someone arrested in West Covina is transported to the West Covina Police Department Jail at 1444 West Garvey Avenue South, West Covina, CA 91790 (phone: 626-939-8500). The West Covina Police Department operates a Type I detention facility at 1444 West Garvey Avenue. It is one of the larger municipal jails in the San Gabriel Valley and handles initial bookings for nearly all West Covina arrests.

For serious felony charges or when the city facility is at capacity, defendants transfer to the Los Angeles County Inmate Reception Center (IRC) at 450 Bauchet Street, Los Angeles, CA 90012. This is the intake point for the Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department (LASD) jail system, which also includes the Men's Central Jail in downtown LA and the Century Regional Detention Facility (CRDF) in Lynwood for female inmates. Angels Bail Bonds posts bonds at every one of these facilities.

West Covina in Detail — What Makes This City's Bail Cases Different

The city sits along the 10 freeway in the eastern San Gabriel Valley, with the 605 on its west side and the 57 east. The useful tip for West Covina cases: bond at WCPD is faster than transferring to LA County IRC, but the transfer window is short on busy weekends. If WCPD jail is at capacity or the charge is felony-level, transport to IRC downtown happens quickly. How a West Covina Bail Bond Actually Works

You call (626) 478-1062. We confirm the bail amount from the Los Angeles County bail schedule — west covina bail bonds typically range from $5,000 for petty theft (PC 484) to $50,000 for domestic violence (PC 273.5) and $100,000 for DUI with injury (VC 23153). You pay Angels Bail Bonds a 10% premium — the state-set rate under California Insurance Code § 1800.4. We post the full bail amount with the jail as a surety bond. The defendant is released, typically 1–4 hours at West Covina PD Jail and 4–8 hours at the Los Angeles County Inmate Reception Center (IRC).

The 10% is a fee for services — it is a premium paid to the bondsman, not a loan. When the case concludes and all court appearances are made, the bond is exonerated.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does a West Covina arrest actually get processed?

West Covina arrests are processed at the WCPD Jail at 1444 W Garvey Avenue South. Misdemeanor bonds post here for fast release. Felonies and overflow transfer to LA County IRC at 441 Bauchet Street downtown.

How much does a bail bond actually cost in West Covina?

The bail bond fee is 10% of the total bail amount under California Insurance Code § 1800.4. For a first-offense DUI with bail set at $15,000, you pay $1,500. That 10% is our fee for posting the full bond — it is not refunded at case conclusion. Payment plans are available for qualifying clients.

Can West Covina bail be posted the same night?

Yes, in most cases. California does not impose a mandatory overnight hold. Once the booking process is complete and the Los Angeles County bail schedule amount is assigned, a licensed bondsman can post the bond immediately. Most West Covina DUI and misdemeanor arrests result in same-night or early-morning release.
